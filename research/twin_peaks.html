Twin Peaks is a TV series that aired two seasons in the early 1990s. It was followed shortly afterwards by a movie, Twin Peaks: Fire Walk With Me, and a handful of books. In 2014, the deleted scenes of the movie were released on their own as Twin Peaks: The Missing Pieces. Finally, in 2017, a third season of the show aired as Twin Peaks: The Return.

The series begins with the death of a high school student in the town of Twin Peaks, a murder case which is investigated by the FBI. If you haven't seen the show, this page is about the last place you want to be.

Consider this a spoiler warning for the entirety of the series.
